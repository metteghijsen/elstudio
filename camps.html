DANCE CAMPS

ElStudio organizes a variety of dance camps every year, designed for people of all ages and levels. These camps are a great opportunity to immerse yourself in dance, make new friends, and have a memorable summer. Whether you're into Salsa, Afro-Cuban rhythms, or just want to stay active, our camps offer something for everyone. They take place in beautiful surroundings and combine intensive dance training with fun activities such as sports, beach outings, and exploring local sights. You don't need a partner to join, and everyone is welcome. Come and spend an unforgettable week with us!

CUBAN SALSA SUMMER CAMP

JULY 21-27, 2024

Join our Cuban Salsa Summer Camp for a week of immersive salsa dancing, with expert instructors and participants from all over the world. You'll get daily salsa lessons, plenty of time to practice, and opportunities to relax and socialize with others. Whether you're a beginner or experienced, this camp is designed to help you improve your skills and have a great time. You don’t need a partner to join. Expect fun parties and performances in the evenings, along with plenty of dancing under the stars!

Find out more

AFRO CUBAN AND RUMBA SUMMER CAMP

JULY 21-27, 2024

Running alongside the Salsa Camp, the Afro Cuban and Rumba Summer Camp offers specialized training in Afro-Cuban dance and Rumba rhythms. It’s an intense but fun camp for those who want to dive deep into these rich dance traditions. The camp takes place in the afternoons, so you can combine it with salsa in the mornings if you like. With live music and a focus on technique, expression, and culture, this camp is ideal for dancers of all levels looking to broaden their skills and knowledge.

Find out more

SUMMER DANCE CAMP

JULY 28 - AUGUST 3, 2024

The Summer Dance Camp is open to everyone who wants to stay active and have fun through dance and sports. With a variety of dance styles taught throughout the week, this camp is perfect for those who want to enjoy a full summer experience. There will be time for relaxation and exploration, including beach trips and visits to nearby towns. If you're looking for an energizing week full of dance, fitness, and outdoor activities, this camp is for you.
