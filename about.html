ABOUT US

At ElStudio, dancing is not just about learning steps—it’s about embracing the joy of movement, connecting with others, and feeling free. We offer a wide range of classes every week for children, teens, young adults, and seniors. There’s no strict dress code, and we encourage you to come as you are. Our atmosphere is welcoming and relaxed, and everyone feels like part of the community. ElStudio is proud to be one of the most affordable dance schools in Copenhagen, offering high-quality instruction from experienced teachers across various styles, all while maintaining a relaxed and friendly environment.

Learn to dance and get better

At ElStudio, we believe that dancing is a continuous journey, where there’s always something new to learn. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced dancer, you can join at any time, even mid-season, and only pay for the remaining weeks. For our couple dancing classes, you don't need a partner; we switch partners regularly so you can dance with multiple people and improve faster. Our instructors bring years of experience, ensuring you get the best guidance. We also offer private lessons, group classes, bachelor/bachelorette parties, and corporate events to help everyone find joy through dance.

Why you should start dancing

Dance is not just a physical activity—it’s a way to build confidence, release stress, and feel alive. At ElStudio, dancing gives you newfound energy, a stronger body, and a clearer mind. Our classes mix fun with technique, offering a wide range of styles like Salsa, Bachata, Hip Hop, Afrobeat, and Jazz, catering to all levels. The social aspect of dancing is a big focus at our school. Whether you’re dancing with a partner or alone, our classes create a positive and engaging space where you can meet new people and make lasting connections.

Events with ElStudio

ElStudio is not just a place to learn dance—it's a community that thrives through participation in exciting events and festivals. Each year, we take part in several local festivals like the Copenhagen Carnival, 48-Hour Festival, and more. These events provide our members with a platform to perform and connect with other dancers. We also organize special events such as club nights for our members and collaborate with guest instructors from around the world for workshops and performances. Our annual dance camp and cultural trips to Cuba offer unforgettable experiences filled with dance, music, and local culture.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Where does the teaching take place?

We have many classes, and therefore, the teaching takes place at multiple locations: Nørrebro (with close proximity between premises), Østerbro, Vesterbro, and Holmen. You can view a complete overview of the premises here.

Is there a dress code?

There is no strict dress code at ElStudio, and you are welcome to come in any clothing or shoes you feel comfortable in. However, we do have some recommendations for specific classes, such as Twerk, Burlesque, Bellydance, and Salsa/Bachata. Please visit our website for detailed recommendations based on the class you're attending.

How do I book a trial lesson?

You can book a trial class if you'd like to try a session before committing. Simply visit our class schedule, select the class you're interested in, and choose the "trial class" option when booking.

How much do different classes cost?

We offer some of the most affordable classes in Copenhagen. Prices vary depending on the class and its duration, with children's classes ranging from 120-240 DKK per month and adult classes typically costing around 60 DKK per session. You can start at any point during the term and only pay for the remaining weeks.

How do I pay?

Payment is made upon registration for each class. The price is listed under each course, and you can pay through our online platform. For children's classes, there's a fixed monthly fee, and discounts are available for signing up for multiple classes.

How do I register?

You can register for classes by selecting the class you're interested in from our schedule and clicking the "Subscribe" button. If this is your first time, you will need to create a profile. Additionally, we charge an annual membership fee of 100 DKK for new members.

Can I be late for class?

We recommend arriving on time to avoid disturbing the class. However, if you're running late, please join as quietly and respectfully as possible.

What if I'm sick or on holiday?

If you're unable to attend a class due to illness or a holiday, please notify us in advance. Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds for missed classes, but you're welcome to join another class as a makeup session if space allows.
