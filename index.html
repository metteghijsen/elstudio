Get to Know El Studio

ElStudio is one of Copenhagen’s most welcoming and versatile dance schools. Offering a variety of styles such as Salsa, Bachata, Hip Hop, Ballet, and more, our mission is to make dance accessible to everyone. Whether you’re a beginner or an advanced dancer, we have something for you. Our talented instructors create a relaxed atmosphere where you can have fun while improving your skills. There’s no dress code—just come as you are. Sign up for one of our classes today and join a community of dance enthusiasts of all ages.

SALSA, BACHATA, TANGO,
KIZOMBA, STANDARD & LATIN

We were the first school to introduce Salsa Classes in Denmark and are still going strong.

DANCE CLASSES

We offer dance classes all over Copenhagen in many types of dance styles.

KIDS & TEEN CLASSES

We have dance classes for kids in all ages. We have many different styles to choose from.

DANCE CAMPS

ElStudio offers annual dance camps for those wanting an immersive experience. Enjoy intensive dance training in Salsa, Bachata, Afro-Cuban rhythms, and more in a relaxed summer setting.

TRIAL CLASSES

Not sure if a class is for you? Try out one of our trial classes before committing to a full session. Get a feel for the dance and the atmosphere.

BOOK US

Book ElStudio for private lessons, corporate events, bachelorette parties, or dance performances. We tailor the event to your needs for an unforgettable experience.

DANCE EVENTS AND WORKSHOPS

We host workshops and dance events for beginners to advanced dancers. Join us for fun and enriching dance experiences, taught by top-notch instructors.
