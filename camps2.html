if you like Salsa, Son, Bachata, Rumba, Afro and Cuban rhythms, this is where it happens! Come and get a lot of inspiration at Bosei Idrætshøjskole on your summer holiday by Præstø, South Zealand, in Denmark. We have held the Salsa Summer Camp since 2004, and have classes for all levels. Everyone can participate, singles and couples.

Join us for an experience for life, meet new people, and learn a lot of dance. When you have first tried our camp, we guarantee that you will get hooked.

How does a day at camp look like?

At our Camp in week 30, there will be 6 different levels: Improver, Intermediate 1, Intermediate 2, Advanced 1, Advanced 2 and Very advanced. And in the afternoon, we will also have master classes.

In the afternoon we will have more levels also master class workshops. And this year we will again have extra classes and workshops on top of the normal program, with Afro Cuban Dance and Rumba

There are 3 hours of daily classes in the morning and 2-4 hours of workshops in the afternoon, with breaks 2-3 times a day. The only classes you need to attend, is the ones in the morning from 9:45-13:00. The rest of the program and the workshops in the afternoon from 14:00-18:00 are optional. If you only like to take afrocuban ans rumba classes in the afternoon you do not need to take part of the moning Salsa classes. All the classes and levels will take part in different dance rooms.
