Jazz & Modern

Our Jazz and Modern classes offer a dynamic blend of styles, allowing dancers to build a strong technical foundation while unlocking their creative potential. Drawing inspiration from Jazz, Modern, Ballet, and more, we place an emphasis on grace, flexibility, strength, and control.

The exercises should motivate the dancers to get to know their body better and thereby increase the awareness of centre stability and weight placement.

What can you expect in the class?

Each class begins with a comprehensive warm-up, carefully designed to activate your muscles, improve posture, and increase your range of motion. From there, you'll explore fluidity in movement and experiment with rhythm, weight shifts, and timing as you dance across the studio.
