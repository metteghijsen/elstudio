Jazz & Modern

Our Jazz and Modern classes offer a dynamic blend of styles, allowing dancers to build a strong technical foundation while unlocking their creative potential. Drawing inspiration from Jazz, Modern, Ballet, and more, we place an emphasis on grace, flexibility, strength, and control. The exercises motivate dancers to know their body better and increase awareness of centre stability and weight placement.

What can you expect in the class?

Each class begins with a comprehensive warm-up designed to activate muscles, improve posture, and increase your range of motion. From there, you'll explore fluidity in movement and experiment with rhythm, weight shifts, and timing.

Book Your Jazz & Modern Class at EL Studio Today!

Ready to discover your energy and rhythm? Sign up for our Jazz class today! Whether you’re a beginner or looking to refine your skills, our classes offer the perfect blend of technique, fun, and expression.
