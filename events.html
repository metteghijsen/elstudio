UPCOMING EVENTS

Join us for a variety of exciting dance workshops and events throughout the year! Whether you're a beginner or an experienced dancer, our events provide a fun way to enhance your skills, meet new people, and enjoy the vibrant energy of dance. Check out our upcoming events below.

SEPTEMBER

WORKSHOPS WITH SAUL

SEPTEMBER 28

Join Saul for an intensive Salsa workshop! This event is perfect for improvers and intermediate dancers looking to sharpen their footwork and body movement. The workshop covers Salsa footwork, bodywork, and couple work. No partner required, but you're welcome to bring one.

Location: Kildevæld Kulturhus, Copenhagen.

WORKSHOPS WITH DANGER

SEPTEMBER 28

Danger and Marielkis bring you an advanced Salsa workshop. This session focuses on couple work for intermediate to advanced dancers. A chance to refine your dance technique with top Cuban instructors.

Location: Kildevæld Kulturhus, Copenhagen.

WORKSHOPS WITH DEMIS

SEPTEMBER 28

This open-level Bachata workshop led by Demis is perfect for anyone interested in improving their Bachata skills. You’ll work on body movement, musicality, and partner dancing techniques.

Location: Kildevæld Kulturhus, Copenhagen.

LEONI TORRES LIVE IN CONCERT

SEPTEMBER 28

Don't miss out on the chance to see Cuban singer Leoni Torres live in concert as part of his Europe Tour 2024! After the concert, join us for an exciting dance party with a live DJ.

Location: Amager Bio, Copenhagen.
